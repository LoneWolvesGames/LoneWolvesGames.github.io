The Happy Hippogriff
2024


After years of adventuring you’ve decided that you want a better life for yourself and want to open up a Tavern.

After saving all of your Platinum, Gold, and Silver you finally have enough to open The Happy Hippogiff! Adventuring may have helped you survive the worst but running a tavern is no walk in the dark and creepy dungeon.

You’ll have to deal with customers demands whether they ask you for a healing or poisoning drink or just not paying their tab at all!

Remember the clients you keep are only as good as the bar they are in! And your play style will determine what your reputation is!

You can cater to the seedy underworld of criminals for money! But they’ll probably run for the hills with your coin in tow!

Or you can be the friendly barkeeper and get your money!
